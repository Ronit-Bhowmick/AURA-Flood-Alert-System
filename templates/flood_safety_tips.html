Flood Safety Guide

Your comprehensive guide to staying safe during floods.

General Safety Tips

  • Stay informed about the weather conditions in your area.
  • Create an emergency plan with your family.
  • Prepare an emergency kit with essential supplies.
  • Know the evacuation routes in your area.

Flood Safety Tips

  • Move to higher ground immediately if there's a flood warning.
  • Avoid walking or driving through floodwaters.
  • Stay away from power lines and electrical wires.
  • Listen to the advice of local authorities.
